What is Predict?

Predict is a tool that helps patients and doctors decide on whether to have conservative or radical management for non-metastatic prostate cancer.

What is Predict:Prostate?

Predict is a tool that helps patients and doctors decide on whether to have conservative or radical management for non-metastatic prostate cancer.

How do I use Predict?

Enter the details about yourself and your prostate cancer, and then select conservative management or radical treatment to see estimates of how effective they are.

We recommend patients read the patient information section before using the tool. Predict is only intended for use amongst men for whom both conservative and radical treatment could be appropriate options

What will Predict tell me?

The Predict tool shows you how different management strategies affect the percentage of men that survive ten and fifteen years after diagnosis.
